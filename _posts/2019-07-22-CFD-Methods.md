## What is CFD?
Computational Fluid Dynamics is a form of digital aerodynamical analysis.

CFD uses heavy numerical analysis and data structures and processing to analyse. Computers are used to perform the complex calculations required to allow the fluid to be simulated in a free-stream environment as well as the interactions between the fluids with surfaces, and therefore demand high performance supercomputers to achieve better solutions.
