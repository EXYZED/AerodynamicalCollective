### [Airfoil](#Airfoil)
>Also known as aerofoil, foil.

is the XZ cross-sectional shape of a aerodynamic device (i.e. a wing blade)

### [Angle of Attack](#Angle of Attack)
> Also known as AoA, Attack angle.

The immediate angle between the chord of a [Airfoil](#Airfoil) and the free-stream flow.

### [Camber](#Camber)
The **asymmetry** between two **acting** surfaces of an [airfoil](https://aerodynamical-collective.com/2019/06/03/Disambiguation.html#Airfoil).
